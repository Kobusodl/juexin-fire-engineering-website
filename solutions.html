Featured specialist solution

Dust Hazard Analysis (DHA)

For industrial and processing facilities, combustible dust can create severe fire and explosion risk. Juexin’s DHA support focuses on identifying combustible dust hazards, ignition sources, accumulation points, process vulnerabilities and engineered mitigation options.

  • Combustible dust risk evaluation
  • Ignition source and accumulation review
  • Protection, housekeeping and mitigation recommendations
  • Support for operational resilience and compliance discussions
